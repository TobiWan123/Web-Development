What are Frameworks?


You can imagine Frameworks like a frame of a Picture which keeps everything in place and determines a form. The picture in the frame has to match the conditions the frame pretends. Furthermore the frame provides everything the picture, or imagine a project, should have and if you happen do to do the same project (like Websites, Games, etc.) over and over, you probably understand how usefull it is, if the base of the project is already set wtih a framework. The Big-Win of frameworks is that the features which they provide are standardized, easy to implement, proofed by many people and on point.



What is Bootstrap?


Bootstrap is a Framework which is based on the styling language CSS and the scripting language HTML. It contains HTML and CSS-based design templates for typography, forms, buttons, tables, grid systems, navigation and other surface design elements as well as additional, optional JavaScript extensions.



Can I customize my product after delivery?


Of course you can. Many people ask me this question, and it always depends of how much experience you have with the understanding of "how the World Wide Web is built". In any project you like, I can implement an Admin-Tool which provides all the features to customize certain parts of your Website/Webapplication really easy. This is similar to Webbuilders, but because it focuses on certain parts, it is often even easier and more clear to use. If you want full control, I can always give a quick look into the code of your product to make some changes by yourself. I am also always available for support after delivery.



When should I use a Website-Builder like Wordpress, Shopify, etc.?


If you're on of the persons who like to design and build by yourself. If you need for example a Webshop or something like an Webapplication with a little greater effort and your maybe in the starting phase of your buisness, a Webbuilder can be a really good tool for you to get started. On the other side if you just need a nice litte Website for your web-presence or a really complex and awesome Webapplikation, maybe because you own a little bigger company or just for personal purpose, I would definetly not recommend to use tools like this. This comes with the reason that many of these tools only provide a limited amount of features for free and you need to pay for additional ones. Also pricing comes with subscriptions and you have to pay an amount every month, whetever if its just a little Add-On or a Styling-Template you like to use. Hosting and other things will be included in this subscription but if you like to have a personal website adress you'll still need to purchase a Domain anyway. And also one of the most important reasons, especially for bigger companys, you do not own your Website unless you got the code for it in your hands and yours alone. Data security also runs always over this third party. And last but not least, much people offer you to build Websites with these tools for nearly the same pricing of an custom made Website other programmers offer you. This is somehow ridiculous, if you keep in my mind that this tools are build to create Websites by yourself. But it also shows that many of these tools like Wordpress are not that easy to use for some people as you would think in the first place.


Notice: there are many ways to host your Website or Webapplication for free.



How does working with me look like?


First of all I would like to take a little chat with you to get to know each other better. This can happen via Skype, Zoom, Discord, Fiverr, or just simple chat or phone. In this conversation we can have an exchange about what your personal ideas and preferences for your project are. After this I will take some time and give you feedback about how I will implement your Website or Webapplication. At this point you can of course tell me if your satisfied with my approach or not really convinced by it. If the second one is the case, you can always decide to cancel your work with me or I will try to find another approach if you like. But if all is ready to go, I will start working on your project and stay in contact with you to give you regulary feedback about your project and talk about further changes or new ideas. The project will be ready in the discussed period and in some cases maybe even earlier. At the end I will present your project and hand it over to you. There is always a money back guarantee if your are not satisfied at this point. After this we cam talk about things like hosting and support for your project or just go with it and handle the rest by yourself.
